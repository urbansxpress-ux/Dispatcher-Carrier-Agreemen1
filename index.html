DISPATCHER–CARRIER AGREEMENT

This Agreement is made and entered into as of the date signed below, by and between:

Dispatcher: John William
Carrier: __________
Date: __________

1. Purpose
The purpose of this Agreement is to set forth the terms under which the Dispatcher will provide load-finding and dispatch services for the Carrier.

2. Services Provided
The Dispatcher agrees to:
• Locate and secure freight loads for the Carrier.
• Communicate with freight brokers, shippers, and other parties on behalf of the Carrier.
• Provide all necessary load information to the Carrier before dispatch.
The Carrier agrees to:
• Operate under their own motor carrier authority (MC#/DOT#).
• Provide accurate and up-to-date truck availability.
• Maintain all required insurance, permits, and licenses.

3. Payment Terms
• The Carrier agrees to pay the Dispatcher 8% of the gross load rate for each load booked through the Dispatcher.
• Payment shall be due within 48 hours of the Carrier receiving payment from the broker/shipper.
• Payments will be made via (CashApp / Zelle / PayPal).

4. Term & Termination
• This Agreement shall remain in effect month-to-month unless terminated by either party with 7 days’ written notice.
• Either party may terminate immediately for breach of contract.

5. Non-Solicitation
The Carrier shall not bypass the Dispatcher to work directly with brokers, shippers, or customers introduced by the Dispatcher, for a period of 6 months after termination of this Agreement.

6. Liability
The Dispatcher is not responsible for damage, loss, delays, or cargo claims.
The Carrier assumes full responsibility for the safe and legal operation of their vehicles.

7. Governing Law
